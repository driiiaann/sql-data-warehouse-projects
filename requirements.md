# Analytics & Reporting Requirements

## Objective
Develop SQL-based analytics to deliver detailed insights into customer behaviour, 
product performance, and sales trends.

---

## Customer Behaviour
- Total customers by country and gender
- Customer distribution by marital status
- Identify high-value customers by total spend

---

## Product Performance
- Top 10 products by total revenue
- Sales breakdown by category and subcategory
- Best performing product lines

---

## Sales Trends
- Monthly and yearly revenue trends
- Average order value over time
- Sales volume by quantity and price

---

## Deliverables
- SQL scripts for each analytical report (see scripts/gold/)
- Power BI dashboard visualising key metrics across all three areas
